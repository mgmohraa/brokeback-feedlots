Larry Mohr — Founder & Boss

Larry started Mohr Farms over 35 years ago with a dream and a strong work ethic. With a deep love for the land and animals, he built the farm from the ground up. Today, Larry still oversees the daily operations and makes sure the values of hard work, sustainability, and family tradition stay strong in everything they do.

Joe Mohr — Oldest Son, Next in Line

Joe grew up working alongside his dad and learning everything about cattle farming. Now taking over day-to-day management, Joe is passionate about modernizing practices while preserving the family’s traditional methods. He’s the future of Mohr Farms and is proud to carry on the legacy.

Chris Mohr — Youngest Son, All-In

Chris joined full-time after college and brings fresh energy to the farm. From managing livestock to marketing local beef, Chris does it all with enthusiasm. He values quality and is excited to grow the business alongside his dad and brother.
